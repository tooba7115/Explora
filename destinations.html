DESTINATIONS

Hunza Valley

The Hunza Valley, along the Hunza River, is known for treks ranging from short hikes to multi-day adventures. Guided tours reveal the valley’s hospitable villages and fresh local apricots. It is especially breathtaking in spring’s cherry blossoms and autumn’s colors, while summers are rainy and winters cold.

K2 Mountian

K2, the world’s second-highest mountain at 8,611 meters, stands proudly in Pakistan’s Karakoram Range. Known as the “Savage Mountain,” Surrounded by glaciers and rugged peaks, it offers awe-inspiring views to trekkers. Many people visit the country just to trek and the tourism industry is well prepared to facilitate all manner of trekking styles.

Swat, Kalam

Swat and Kalam, known as the *“Switzerland of the East,”* are valleys of stunning natural beauty. Snowy peaks, rivers, and green meadows create a breathtaking landscape year-round. Swat offers a blend of history and culture, while Kalam is famed for its serenity. Together, they are perfect for both adventure and relaxation.

Clifton Beach, Karachi

Clifton Beach in Karachi is a popular spot for relaxing walks and vibrant gatherings by the Arabian Sea. It’s especially enjoyable in winter with mild weather and lively picnics, while summers are hot and monsoon rains bring occasional showers.

Nathia Gali

Nathia Gali, nestled in Pakistan’s hills, is famous for its cool weather and scenic trails. Visitors enjoy peaceful walks through pine forests and charming mountain views. It’s especially beautiful in summer when the weather is mild, while winters bring snow and a cozy atmosphere.

Mushkpuri Top

Mushkpuri Top, the highest peak in Nathia Gali, offers stunning panoramic views and peaceful hiking trails. It’s a favorite spot for nature lovers, especially during summer when the weather is cool and clear, while winters bring snow and a tranquil, chilly atmosphere.

Aarang Kel

Arang Kel, a picturesque village in Kashmir, is known for its lush greenery and peaceful mountain views. It’s a great destination for nature walks and fresh air, especially vibrant in summer, while winters bring snow that transforms it into a winter wonderland.

Shangri-la Resort, Skardu

Shangri-La Resort, nestled in the scenic mountains of Kashmir, provides a perfect blend of luxury and nature. Visitors especially enjoy its cool, pleasant summers and cozy, snow-covered winters, making it a year-round retreat. The resort also offers access to nearby hiking trails and local culture, enhancing the overall experience.

Patriata chairlift, Muree

The Patriata Chairlift, located near Murree, is a popular attraction offering stunning views of lush green hills and pine forests. It provides an exciting and scenic ride up the mountains, especially refreshing in the cool summer months. Winters bring a magical snowy landscape, making the experience even more memorable.

Noor Mahal

Noor Mahal, located in Bahawalpur, is a stunning palace known for its grand architecture and rich history. Built in the 19th century, it showcases a blend of European and Mughal styles. It was built in 1872 as an Italian chateau on neoclassical lines, at a time when modernism had set in.
